# On recruiting a passionate developer in the 21st century

- What are the courses that you enjoyed the most during
  the school/university/other ?

    - Why ?
    - Would you like to study in deep these topics in order to
      improve knowledge ?
    - Are there some courses that you would like to repeat ?
        - Why ?

- Do you like to write code and learn new things during your
  spare time ?

    - What is the last article/blog/book that you've read ?
      - Any feedback ?
      - Do you follow any website ? blog ? podcast ? whatever ?

    - What kind of technologies/programming languages are you
      studying right now ?

- What you think code quality stands for ?
- How do you test your code ?

- Are you involved in opensource projects ?

    - Which ones ?
    - How do you started one ?
    - Why do you decided to start ?
    - What you are looking for in opensource projects ?
    - What are the main topics of interest and why ?
    - What is your favourite opensource project and why ?

- Would you like to get involved in opensource projects ?

    - Why you not started yet ?

- What kind of topics would you like to practice on in the near
  future ?

    - Why ?
    - If you have the chance to start practice now, how do you
      thinks to apply this practices ?

        - A pet project ?
        - A spike ?
        - A blog ?
        - An existing project ?

- Do you join communities ?

    - Did you partecipated in conferences ?
        - Which one ?
        - Why ?
        - What conference do you liked most ? Why ?
    - Do you partecipates to local meetups ?
        - Which ones ? Why ?

- Would you like to join local meetups or conferences ?

- Oh, serious now! What the fuck you are looking for ?

    - What you think to find here ?
    - How do think to improve our team ?
    - Are you leaving a crew ?
        - Why ?
        - What did you learned from the past ?
        - What you think to learn from us ?

- Ok, let's write down some code with me !
